Past Woo-Cons

Woo-Con was established in 2014 by COWA!, and the first iteration of the con was held in Spring of 2015. We hope that this event comes back each year, better and better each time! The 2017 Woo-Con was the best one yet, and we can't wait to see how 2018's Woo-Con turns out!

Woo-Con 2017 was featured in the Wooster Daily Record--thank you, Daily Record! Read their spotlight article here. (With a picture gallery!)
