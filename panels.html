Anything I should know about panels at Woo-Con?

  • Some panels may not be appropriate for younger congoers.
  • All panels will have content warnings/descriptions on our website.
  • Any panels with mature/adult discussion content will be held in the late evening/night.
  • Panels have been pre-screened by our staff, but we are not responsible for the content of panels.
  • Some panelists, such as student groups, are affiliated with The College of Wooster, but their views do not necessarily reflect the views of The College.
  • Some of our panelists are not affiliated with the college, and do not necessarily reflect the views of The College as well.

What are panels?

Panels are discussions or any kind of activity which have a leader talking to or instructing a crowd. Panels can be on any topic, and can be a wide variety of things! This year's panel list is still being determined, but we will add it here once we have it!

Some examples of panels in the past include...

  • • An Introduction to Doctor Who
  • • Spotlight on local webseries production
  • • A discussion of consent in shounen-ai manga and anime
  • • Undertale cosplay and discussion
  • • Cosplay makeup tips and tricks
  • • Anime Bada-Bingo!
  • • Local author spotlight
  • • Anime in Context of Japanese Culture / Avoiding Cultural Appropriation
  • • Webseries creation and process

And many more! To check out the 2017 panel schedule, go to our Schedule Page!
