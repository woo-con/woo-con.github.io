Partners & Acknowledgements

Because we can't do it alone!

An image featuring the Woo-Con! 2015 Vendor floor and a performance of a student music group from Bowling Green State University, "GAME".

A big event like Woo-Con! wouldn't be able to happen without plenty of help and commitment from volunteers, staffers, panelists, vendors, and college students with a bit too much free time on their hands.

Also, we would like to give a special thanks to The Wooster Daily Record for writing a spotlight on our 2017 convention, and featuring us both in online and print editions of their newspaper! We're honored! Check it out here.

This page is for the 2017 Woo-Con! We will update with our 2018 partners as we begin to plan the event. In the meantime: Thanks again to all of our partners and volunteers for making the event a huge success!

Partners / Acknowledgements

We would like to thank The College of Wooster for giving us a venue and the resources to put on an event like this! We also would like to thank to College's staff, faculty and administration for aiding us in every step of the way, especially Julia Zimmer and Maria Garcia de la Noceda for helping us with the logistics and the execution of our event. Thank you! Image Credit.

We would also like to thank Funimation Entertainment for generously providing us with screening rights/a screening license for our convention, both for Woo-Con II and this year as well. We really appreciate it! Thank you so much, Funimation!

We would also like to thank one of our partners, Gojotekicon!
Gojotekicon, or "friendly-con," is the only anime, gaming, and comic convention in Ashland, OH.
It will take place May 5-7, 2017 at the Holiday Inn Express, in Ashland, OH.
Registration is $30 for all three days, and $15 per day. Visit www.gojotekicon.com for more information!
